What Members Get Access To

Unhired Interns is built to give people more than motivation. Members get access to a growing space for opportunities, real connections, career direction, projects, events, and updates that make the search less confusing.

Community access

Connect with students, grads, and early-career builders

Meet people who are applying, building, learning, and trying to break in. The goal is to make the journey feel less isolated.

Opportunities

Internships, entry-level roles, and useful leads

Members can share internships, entry-level openings, referrals, resources, and helpful opportunities they come across.

Real-time updates

News and updates for the careers you care about

Get timely updates on career news, industry changes, hiring trends, events, and useful resources connected to your interests.

Career roadmaps

Clear paths for the career you want to enter

Access roadmaps that explain what to learn, what projects to build, what tools matter, and what steps can move you forward.

Company direction

Understand companies and what they look for

Get organized information on companies, roles, skills, application timing, and what different fields may expect from candidates.

Career blogs

Blogs and stories from people already in the field

Read practical blogs, experiences, and lessons from people in the careers members want to enter, so the path feels more real.

Projects

Find collaborators and build real experience

Join or start projects with other members so you can build portfolio work, practice teamwork, and create something meaningful.

Hackathons

Unhired Interns will host its own hackathons

The community will announce and run its own hackathons, giving members a chance to build, compete, learn, and showcase their work.

Career support

Resume, interview, and networking help

Get support with resumes, outreach messages, interview preparation, LinkedIn profiles, confidence, and next steps.
